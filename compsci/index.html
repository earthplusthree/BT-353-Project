Computer Science

This page serves as a hub, connecting prospective and current Computer Science students at Stevens Institute of Technology to university and outside resources s​pecific to their major.

Computer science is concerned with the scientific, engineering, management and mathematical issues in developing and applying software technology in the modern information world. The mission of the Department of Computer Science (CS) is to be a world leader both in education and research by conducting research that produces advancements in computer science for the benefit of society and educating students to become leaders in the development and application of computer science technology. For more information on the Department of Computer Science at Stevens, visit:
https://www.stevens.edu/schaefer-school-engineering-science/departments/computer-science

The Department of Computer Science (CS) offers two undergraduate degrees and minors: computer science and cybersecurity with undergraduate tracks in software systems and computer graphics. Computer science also offers an outstanding master’s program, with degrees in cybersecurity, machine learning, and media & broadcast engineering, in addition to a doctoral program in computer science. Eleven graduate certificates of special study are also available. The department includes world-class research centers and laboratories, including the Center for the Advancement of Secure Systems and Information Assurance (CASSIA).
