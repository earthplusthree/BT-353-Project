Business

This page serves as a hub, connecting prospective and current Business students at Stevens Institute of Technology to university and outside resources s​pecific to their major.

The School of Business at Stevens employs a unique, cross-disciplinary approach to education — emphasizing technology, data and analytics alongside leadership skills — that creates incredible employment opportunities for students. This focus also steers faculty research to address areas of greatest need in industry, keeping curricula ahead of the curve in a fast-changing business world. For more information on the school of business at Stevens, visit:

Whether as interns or early-career professionals, Stevens business students are trusted with high-profile assignments in areas like disruptive technologies, optimization, strategic marketing, predictive analytics and risk assessment, because corporate managers know these skills are at the core of their education. Stevens students also are excellent presenters and collaborators thanks to assignments that require working in teams to manage timelines, complete projects, and report findings to peers and industry guests. School of Business alumni are highly sought after for their ability to leverage data and apply analytical insights in solving problems. Stevens students learn to think critically about technology as they learn new tools and find creative ways to use them in real business settings. Our alumni go on to exciting careers at Verizon, Goldman Sachs, Apple, EY, Google, Barclays, BD and other Fortune 500 companies, in nearby Manhattan and beyond, where they are able to make smarter decisions faster thanks to an understanding of how to mine, visualize and analyze data with great sophistication and efficiency.
