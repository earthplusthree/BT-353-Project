College of Arts and Letters

This page serves as a hub, connecting prospective and current C.A.L students at Stevens Institute of Technolsogy to university and outside resources s​pecific to their major.

The College of Arts and Letters engages in research and scholarship at the intersection of science, technology, arts, and humanities. The goal is to inspire everyone at Stevens—the musicians and artists, the engineers and technologists, the entrepreneurs and scientists—to make positive, long-lasting impacts on society and the world. For more information on the College of Arts and Letters at Stevens, visit:
https://www.stevens.edu/college-arts-and-letters

The College of Arts and Letters (CAL) offers fourteen undergraduate degrees and minors: Data Visualization, Film Studies, Gender & Culture Studies, History, Literature, Medical Humanities, Music & Technology, Philosophy, Pre-Law & Public Policy, Science Communication, Science, Technologies, & Society, Social Sciences, Theater & Technology, and Visual Arts & Technology. A Computational Social Science Graduate Certificate is also available. The department includes world-class research centers and laboratories, including the Sound Synthesis Research Center and Sensory Computation, Experimental Narrative Environments (SCENE) Lab
